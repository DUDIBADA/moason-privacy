MOASON

Privacy Policy — Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Overview

MOASON ("we", "our", "the app") is a competitive tap game developed by Dudibada. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we protect it.

2. Data We Collect

  • Google Account: Email address, display name, and profile photo — used for authentication and leaderboard display.
  • Location (optional): City, state/region, and country — used for regional leaderboards and the world map. Your exact GPS coordinates are never shared with other users. We apply a random offset (~500m) to protect your privacy. You can disable map visibility in Settings.
  • Game Data: Scores, streaks, clicks, and energy — stored to track your progress and rankings.
  • Device Info: Basic device identifiers for anti-cheat detection only.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • Display your name and score on leaderboards
  • Show regional activity on the world map
  • Calculate daily rewards for top players
  • Detect and prevent cheating

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud functions. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob: Rewarded ads for energy. AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization. Google Privacy Policy
  • CARTO / OpenStreetMap: Map tiles for the world map view. No personal data is sent to these services.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore (europe-west1 region). We use Firebase Security Rules to ensure users can only read/write their own data. Scores are validated server-side to prevent tampering.

6. Your Rights

  • Access: You can view all your data in the app (Profile screen).
  • Location: You can update or remove your location at any time in Profile settings.
  • Map Visibility: You can hide your dot from the map in Settings ("Show me on map" toggle).
  • Deletion: To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

MOASON is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us for removal.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
