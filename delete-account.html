Account & Data Deletion

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your MOASON account and all associated data, send an email to:

dudibada@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your MOASON account.

What Gets Deleted

  • Your profile (name, email, photo)
  • All game data (scores, streaks, clicks)
  • Location data (city, region, country, coordinates)
  • Ad watch history
  • Reward history

Timeline

Account deletion will be processed within 7 business days of receiving your request.

Data Retained

After deletion, no personal data is retained. Anonymized aggregate data (regional score totals) may remain as it cannot be linked back to your account.
